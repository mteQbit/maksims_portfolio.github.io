About project

This was done with collaboration with MST(Medisch Spectrum Twente), hospital located in Enschede. They wanted to see possibilities of VR in education. So about the aggression management training in VR. Currently this training’s are involving actor to participate in this, which obviously costs decent amount of money together with logistics, and can’t be done very often. So they wanted to know, is it possible to replace this training’s with VR experience.

Results

At the end of the project, as a team of four we delivered, almost fully recreated MST waiting room, from photos, created one virtual human and animated him. As end result we got about 1 minute demo of virtual character being aggressive towards trainee, due to delay. In the video below you can see some camera shots from the scene and character it self. It just a trailer not demo itself.
