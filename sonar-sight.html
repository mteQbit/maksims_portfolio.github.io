About project

During last quart of second year we had two month project. This was serious game development for customer. Our customer was Oyfo museum located in Hengelo. They wanted to have a possibility to explain to children how different waves in different frequencies works, so every team was free to choose, which frequency to use. My team decided to explain how sonar is working.

About the Game

You are playing as fish in underwater. Environment around you is dark, and you can barely navigate, so fish sends out sound /sonar waves. Whenever wave riches some surfaces, it stops going further and surface got highlighted. In order to proceed in game you need to eat small fishes and avoid big once. Once you eat enough fishes, you grow up and from that moment can also eat bigger fishes. After you became biggest fish in the level, you can proceed to another level. There are 3 levels in total.
