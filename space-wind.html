About project

This was a first project in 3d space and it took place in the end of first year. We were group of 5 people and we had 3 weeks for it. We created a endless runner, where you are playing as star-ship in space and you have to defend your self from enemies just by killing them. Each wave more enemies will come.

About the Game

You are controlling space ship. From movements you can accelerate, break and rotate. In order to proceed in game you need to kill enemies by shooting with blasters in front of your space ship. After you defeat wave, you will get short rest and than new wave will start. Also you have a sort of HP bar which is representing your current shield status. Every time you got hit by enemies blaster or you collide with asteroid, margin of safety of your shield will decrease.
